Project Vision
==============

Create the world's first AI Building Risk Intelligence Platform.

The platform continuously archives, verifies, and analyzes
government building enforcement records before they disappear,
then produces AI-powered risk reports for lenders, insurers,
developers, investors and property buyers.

Target Markets

• Australia
• United States
• United Kingdom
• Canada
• Singapore

---------------------------------------------------------
Problem
---------------------------------------------------------

Government agencies regularly publish:

• Rectification Orders
• Fire Orders
• Stop Work Orders
• Defect Notices
• Dangerous Building Notices

However,

• Some records disappear.
• URLs change.
• PDFs are removed.
• Councils use different systems.
• Data cannot easily be searched.

Financial institutions therefore cannot easily determine
whether a building has hidden regulatory risks.

---------------------------------------------------------
Solution
---------------------------------------------------------

Create an AI platform that

1. Crawls government websites continuously.

2. Stores every document.

3. Creates immutable evidence.

4. Uses AI to summarize every order.

5. Builds Builder Risk Profiles.

6. Produces Building Risk Scores.
