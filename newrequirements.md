Prysmian – AI-Powered RAG Chatbot for Factory Operations

We developed an AI-powered self-service knowledge base chatbot for Prysmian, built with Microsoft Power Apps, Azure AI, and Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) technology.

Designed for use in a manufacturing environment, the solution enables factory workers to access technical knowledge completely hands-free. Instead of typing, operators can simply wear a headset and ask questions about machines, equipment, troubleshooting procedures, or operating instructions using natural speech.

The chatbot converts speech to text, uses Azure AI Speech Services to understand the request, and leverages RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) to retrieve the most relevant information from the company's internal knowledge base. The AI then generates an accurate, context-aware response and delivers it back to the worker through text-to-speech, allowing them to continue working without interrupting their tasks.

Key Technologies

Microsoft Power Apps
Azure AI Speech (Speech-to-Text & Text-to-Speech)
Azure OpenAI
Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG)
Enterprise Knowledge Base Integration
Business Benefits

Hands-free access to technical documentation
Faster machine troubleshooting and issue resolution
Reduced downtime on the production floor
Improved knowledge sharing and consistency
Enhanced productivity and worker safety through voice-first interaction
